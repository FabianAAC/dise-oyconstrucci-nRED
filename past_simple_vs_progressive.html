PAST SIMPLE VS PROGRESSIVE

Past simple is mainly used to describe habits, routines and actions that were completed at a specific time in the past. We tend to use frequency adverbs, or specific times in the past.

* I had a quiz last night

* I always went to school at 6am

Past continuous is mainly used to describe an action happening at the moment, usually interrupted by another action.

* I was eating pizza when my mom called me.

* I was travelling to Barranquilla when my boyfriend had an accident.
