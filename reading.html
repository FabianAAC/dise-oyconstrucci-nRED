"Once I cut my toe"

It was back in 1999, a very sunny and beautiful day in Bucaramanga, Colombia, a young 5-year old boy whose main objective in life was playing soccer and riding bicycle. That day, I was playing soccer while my brother was riding his bicycle,so he invited me over:" Fabián, come here with me", I said yes. In that moment I thought that would be a cool idea, but suddenly it turned into the worst idea ever. When I put my foot on the wheel (barefoot to make it worse), he did not give a second to think but started to ride the bike instead. After that, all I remember is me calling  my mother´s name with the  most deafening voice ever, so she  went out right away and asked."What happened"; in shock, my brother just answered: "Nothing mom, we were just playing and riding the bike when the accident happened",.."What acciden" - she asked. My terrified brother answered, " Fabian cut his toe while riding the bicycle with me". Next, my mom called a taxi and ten minutes later I was at the hospital waiting to be assisted. The beautiful nurse washed my foot and took care of the situation.. I was shaking and crying, while my brother was talking to my mom trying to find a valid excuse for the situation.
